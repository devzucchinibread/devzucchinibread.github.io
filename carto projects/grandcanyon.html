The Grand Canyon (North Rim)

This shaded relief map highlights the dramatic topography of the Grand Canyon’s North Rim in Arizona, USA. Using a digital elevation model (DEM) as the base, the terrain has been styled with naturalistic hillshading and land cover symbology to emphasize the canyon’s steep walls, ridgelines, and plateaus.The muted earth tones convey elevation change—from the deep reds and browns of the inner canyon to the soft greens of the surrounding highlands.

Tools Used:

ArcGIS Pro, ENVI, Adobe Illustrator
